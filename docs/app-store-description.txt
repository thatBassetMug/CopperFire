Subtitle: Zen painting with light

Description:
Touch the darkness and watch copper flames bloom beneath your fingertip. CopperFire is a meditative drawing canvas where light emerges from nothing.

Press and hold anywhere to ignite a glowing brush stroke that shifts through warm copper, deep orange, rose, violet, indigo, and teal. The longer you hold, the further the colors travel. Pinch to change your brush size. Every mark you make lingers on the canvas, layered with screen-blended light.

No accounts. No tutorials. No undo. Just you and the flame.
