CopperFire

A zen painting canvas. Press and hold to ignite copper flame brush strokes that shift through warm copper, rose, violet, and deep teal.

Privacy Policy

CopperFire does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device with no network connections, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party services.

Your creations exist only on your screen and are not saved or shared unless you choose to take a screenshot.
